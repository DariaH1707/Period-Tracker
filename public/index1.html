About SS Period Tracker

The SS (Syntax Sisters) period tracker is a convenient tool designed to help individuals monitor and manage their menstrual cycles effectively. Users input various data points such as the start and end dates of their periods, mood fluctuations, intensity of cramps, cravings, energy levels, and blood collection method. With this information, the tracker calculates the length of each menstrual cycle and provides insightful facts about periods.
