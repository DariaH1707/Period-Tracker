About SS Period Tracker

The SS (Syntax Sisters) period tracker is a convenient tool designed to help individuals monitor and manage their menstrual cycles effectively. Users input various data points such as the start and end dates of their periods, mood fluctuations, intensity of cramps, cravings, energy levels, and blood collection method. With this information, the tracker calculates the length of each menstrual cycle and provides insightful facts about periods.

Why use?

SS Period tracker serves as a valuable companion in:

Navigating the complexities of menstrual health

This aids in understanding the intricacies of one's cycle, including variations in mood, energy levels, and physical symptoms


Promoting self-awareness

This helps individuals recognize patterns and responses to menstruation, empowering individuals to make informed choices about their health and lifestyle

And informed decision-making regarding one's well-being

This is facilitated by access to comprehensive data and knowledge about menstrual health, enabling individuals to advocate for their needs and seek appropriate support and resources
